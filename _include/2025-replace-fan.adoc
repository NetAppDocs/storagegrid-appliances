// Replace fan in SG6200-CN, SGF6212, SG120, SG1200
// Intro, before you begin, about this task, and after you finish are in referencing topic

.Steps
. Wrap the strap end of the ESD wristband around your wrist, and secure the clip end to a metal ground to prevent static discharge.
. Locate the fan that you need to replace.
+
The seven fans are in the following positions in the chassis (front half of StorageGRID appliance with top cover removed shown):

. Using the blue tabs on the fan, lift the failed fan out of the chassis.
+
image:../media/drw_s2025_fan_replace_ieops-2552.svg[Removing a fan from the chassis]
. Slide the replacement fan into the open slot in the chassis.
+
Align the connector on the fan with the socket in the circuit board.
+
. Press the fan's connector firmly into the circuit board.
